Platform Capabilities

Everything you need to automate AI workflows at scale

Built for teams that demand reliability, security, and full operational control over their AI infrastructure.

Infrastructure as Code

Define workflows in YAML with version-controlled lifecycle. Validate offline, plan changes with diffs, and apply atomically — just like Terraform.

Checkpoint & Resume

Every step checkpoints to Postgres. If a process crashes, execution resumes from the last completed step — no lost work, no duplicate operations.

Bring Your Own Keys

Use your own API keys for every provider. No proxy fees, no vendor lock-in, no third party touching your data. Full cost transparency and control.

Single Binary Deploy

One binary, one Postgres instance. No Kafka, no Redis, no container orchestrators required. Ship to production in minutes, not weeks.

Full Audit Trail

Every state-changing operation emits an audit event from day one. Know who did what, when, and why. Built for compliance requirements.

Extensible via gRPC

Build custom connectors as gRPC plugins running as subprocesses. Extend Mantle with your own integrations without forking the core.

Workflow Lifecycle

From YAML to production in four commands

A familiar IaC lifecycle that your platform team already understands. No new paradigms to learn.

1
mantle validate

Validate

Offline schema validation. Catch errors in CI before they reach production.

2
mantle plan

Plan

Diff against the applied version. See exactly what will change before committing.

3
mantle apply

Apply

Atomically apply the versioned workflow definition. Fully audited, rollback-ready.

4
mantle run

Run

Execute with checkpointing, retry, timeout, and crash recovery built in.

Zero Trust Architecture

Your keys never leave your infrastructure

Mantle treats secrets as opaque handles. Credentials are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM and resolved only at connector invocation time. They are never exposed in expressions, logs, or API responses.

  • API keys encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM
  • Secrets resolved at invocation, never in CEL expressions
  • No proxy layer — direct connection to providers
  • Full cost transparency with your own billing
  • Multi-tenancy and RBAC on the roadmap

Security First

Built for teams that take security seriously

Every design decision in Mantle prioritizes security, auditability, and operational control.

Opaque Secret Handles

Credentials are never exposed as raw values. The engine resolves secrets at connector invocation time, keeping them out of logs and expressions.

Encryption at Rest

All stored credentials use AES-256-GCM authenticated encryption. Keys managed through your own infrastructure and rotation policies.

Immutable Audit Log

Every state-changing operation is recorded. Who changed what, when, and the full before/after state. Meet compliance requirements from day one.
